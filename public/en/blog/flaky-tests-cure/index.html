Flaky tests: fix them, do not hide them

Flakes kill trust in automation. Here is how to eliminate them systematically.

Back to all posts

Flaky tests are more than annoyance. They tax the team and destroy confidence in automation.

Do not hide flakes with retries. Fix the root cause.

Common sources

  • UI races and weak waits.
  • Network delays and third-party services.
  • Shared state between tests.

A practical cure

  1. Add a flaky label and a separate backlog.
  2. Collect diagnostics: video, logs, screenshots.
  3. Remove time dependencies and use explicit conditions.

Conclusion

A stable test beats a fast test. Invest in the health of your framework.
