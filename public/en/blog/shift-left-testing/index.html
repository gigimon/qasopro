Shift-left testing without pain: how to start

Shift-left is not a phase, it is a change in how the team communicates.

Back to all posts

Shift-left often gets translated as “test earlier”. In practice it means involving QA in analysis, design, and planning.

The main effect is fewer defects reaching late stages and a faster feedback loop.

Quick steps

  1. Bring QA into refinement to discuss risks.
  2. Add lightweight checks in PRs (linters, contract tests).
  3. Test specifications, not only implementations.

What does not work

Adding extra QA workload without changing team processes. Shift-left is a culture shift, not a bigger backlog.
