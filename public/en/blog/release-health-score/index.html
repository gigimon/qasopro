Release Health Score: measuring release readiness

A single indicator that helps teams decide if a release is ready.

Back to all posts

Teams often look at dozens of signals before a release and still miss the big picture. Release Health Score compresses the situation into one indicator.

The key is transparency and shared understanding across the team.

What goes into the score

  • Percentage of critical tests passed.
  • Number of blocking defects.
  • Environment and service stability.

How to use it

Treat the score as a traffic light, not as the only release decision. It should support judgment, not replace it.

Conclusion

A health score increases predictability and reduces “release by luck” moments.
